Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our programs, admissions, and support services.

How quickly can I start a course?

You can start immediately after enrollment. Most courses have flexible start dates, and we offer both self-paced and live instructor-led options. Contact us to discuss the best schedule for your needs.

Do you offer financial assistance?

Yes, we offer various payment plans, scholarships for deserving students, and EMI options. We also have special discounts for early birds and group enrollments. Contact our advisors for personalized assistance.

What support do you provide after course completion?

We provide comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, job placement assistance, and continued mentorship. Our alumni network and industry connections help you advance your career.

Are classes available online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person classes to accommodate different learning preferences and schedules. Our hybrid model allows you to switch between formats as needed. All sessions are recorded for later review.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the course quality. Terms and conditions apply. We're confident in our programs and want to ensure your complete satisfaction.

How experienced are your instructors?

Our instructors are industry professionals with 5+ years of experience working at top tech companies. They bring real-world expertise and stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies.
